# [AI in Healthcare: Lessons from Oncology Innovation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QNCh690q9ng)
**Speaker :** [Asba Tasneem](https://www.linkedin.com/in/asbatasneem/)
### What is HealthCare AI ?
Application of ML & NLP technologies to improve various aspects of healthcare
- Diagnostics
- Treatment
- Patient Care
- Drug discovery
- Operational Efficiencies
### Key Ingredients to develop AI technology
* Data Access
* Collaboration
* Domain Expertise
* Funding
* Regulatory Compliance
### Developing an AI-enabled Medical Device
**Oncology use case :** Automating RECIST (Comparing the sizes of the tumors in CT scan over different periods to conclude whether the tumor is growing, staying the same or reducing in size)
1. Data Collection
2. Gold Standard Dataset
3. AI model development
4. Fine-tuning and evaluation
5. Productization & Validation
6. Regulatory Clearance 
7. Deployment and Monitoring

## Income Split
- 30 % - Fixed monthly basic needs (House rent, RentMojo bills, WiFi, Mobile recharge)
- 30 % - Personal Joy / Lifestyle (Travel, shopping, food etc.)
- 40 % - Savings

> Because **money isn’t the end goal — freedom and fulfillment are.**
- If I mostly save but feel constrained, I may later **resent the process**
- If I spend mostly, I might **miss out on compounding it**
